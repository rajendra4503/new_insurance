Managed by Experts

Your doctor's expertise is leveraged to ensure that you are reminded in a timely manner. Your doctor, nutritionist, pharmacist, or gym instructor, know the right time to do the right things that will help you the most.

Reminders & Followups

Forgot to take the insulin injection or the eye-drops for glaucoma. Don't worry eTPA will remind you. If you still forget, your loved ones will be informed and then they can call you to remind you to take your medicines.

Notifications & Alarms

Non-critical reminders are sent as notifications. These discreet messages won't interrupt your meeting, but will let you know what you have to do. The Critical reminders are loud so miss the medication.

Plans

Plans are schedules of activities, reminders and notifications, created by experts (doctors, nutritionists, or pharmacists) who specialize in a particular area. These plans can be customized to a specific people's needs.

Activities

The activities planned for you need you to set aside a specific amount of time e.g. taking a walk or doing yoga. These activities are also updated in the phone's calender so that you don't schedule anything else at that time.

Appointments

Appointments are special activities for which the user is reminded three times before the appointment time, so that the user can confirm, reschedule or cancel the appointment if needed.

Video/Audio/Document Links

In case the planner feels that you could benefit from viewing a video to understand how to do an activity, he can embed a video link, which can be viewed right on your mobile. So can a document or an audio file.

Important Numbers Directory

This feature allows you to store important numbers that you can quickly locate and call in case of an emergency. Record your doctor's number, nearest hospital's number, emergency services number

Data Capture for Compliance

Many times, track the compliance is important. For example, a reminder to a user to measure his fasting blood sugar in the morning and then enter the value of the glucometer reading would help in tracking the compliance.

Data Analytics for Plan-User & Plan-Provider

Every user whose compliance data is being captured can view their data analytics to understand how they are doing. Every doctor can view the data analytics for all their plan-users, to measure their progress or lack of it.

Social Media Feeds

A plan builder can publish relevant updates and other material for plan-users on their Facebook page. Every plan user will get this update in the Social media feed in their app.

Your Document Folder

Now you can store all your prescriptions, lab reports etc. on your mobile phone. Ask your lab to forward your lab results to [your registered mobile @planpiper.com]. Make sure that you include your country code.
