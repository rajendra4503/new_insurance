Benefits


For Healthcare providers

  • Very high degree of customer engagement with low costs for follow-ups.
  • Can be integrated with hospital management system. So no need of double data entry.
  • No capital expenditure.
  • Assurance of high success rates due to increased medication adherence.
  • Increase in revenue for doctors by reducing patient drop-outs.
  • Increased revenue for healthcare provider as patient reminded to meet the doctor periodically to review their health parameters.
 

For patients

  • Patients receive the full benefit of healthcare they deserve by following the timely notifications and alarms. They ensure that one is reminded in a timely manner to take their medicine, measure their blood sugar, go for a walk, set up an appointment and much more.
